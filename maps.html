Grizzly Base Camp

Grizzly Base Camp is one of the four camp properties owned and operated by Montana Council BSA, and is managed by the Grizzly Base Camp Committee. The camp completely surrounds McGilvray Lake and offers nearly 100 acres of wilderness for scouts to learn and test their skills.
