Contact Us

We would like to know how we can help you

If you are interested in contacting us directly, please take a moment to fill out the form below. Your submission will be sent to the Scoutmaster's email address. Please allow a few days for a reply, either by phone or email.

Certain fields are required. For instance, your name and email address will be required, along with the text you are sending in the email. You must also fill in the Captcha field in order to submit your text.

Privacy Policy

Troop 1933 is not a business for profit. Its leadership and members have no interest in acquiring personal details, like email addresses and phone numbers, nor will any contact information be sold or given to third parties. All submissions from this online form are sent to a Gmail account, where they are automatically destroyed after 30 days. Troop 1933 takes necessary precautions to maintain adequate privacy for its members and partners.
